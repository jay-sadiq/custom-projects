Trip Planner — AI family trip companion

Plan and follow AI-generated itineraries on your phone. Trip Planner connects to your Trip Planner account and lets you:

• View trips, day-by-day schedules, and maps
• Create new AI-planned trips
• Edit day notes and checklist items
• Upload photos to stops
• Import bookings from confirmation text
• Use cached data offline when traveling

Requires a Trip Planner backend account. The web app provides full calendar editing; the mobile app is optimized for on-trip use.

Privacy policy: see PRIVACY.md in the repository or the URL provided in store settings.
